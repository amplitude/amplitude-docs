{{markdown}} ## Troubleshooting For more information about individual statuses and errors, see the [Session Replay Ingestion Monitor](/docs/session-replay/ingestion-monitor). ### Styles, fonts, drawables, images don't appear in replay (alpha) When Amplitude captures a replay, it doesn't transfer or store styles or other static assets that are part of your application.
